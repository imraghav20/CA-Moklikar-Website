About Us

Swati is a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). she is also a Bachelor of Commerce from the prestigious Rajashri Shahu College (Latur) she has more than 8 years of professional experience.

Our expertise lies in the fields of audit, taxation, accounts & finance. We offer expert services to public and private businesses houses and to individuals as well. Our experienced and professionally focused team members are dedicated to provide efficient & prompt services in a consistent manner.

We have a strong portfolio of offerings for our esteemed clients to match their needs, the services which they expect from a leading group of finance professionals, on which they can completely rely upon & which will help them gain competitive advantage in todays' competitive business world. Her experience includes business & operational risk advisory, GST audit, GST Compliance, Setting up of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), etc. She has a special inclination towards GST wherein he likes to write, read, research and speak about GST and previous Indirect Taxes generally as well as on a case to case basis. A thinker, innovator with a problem-solving attitude often helps us to provide a good overall solution. A firm believer in adding value to the clients irrespective of their size.
